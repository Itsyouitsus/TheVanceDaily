The Vance Daily - April 03, 2026

President Trump announced Vice President JD Vance will serve as the administration's new "fraud czar," focusing investigations on alleged misuse of federal funds in Democratic-controlled states.

Trump Names Vance 'Fraud Czar': The President designated Vance to lead a nationwide crackdown on alleged fraud, with particular emphasis on blue states and federal fund misuse.

Vance Announces New Memoir: The Vice President revealed plans for a second book titled "My Way Back to Faith," detailing his spiritual journey.

Hungary Diplomatic Mission: Trump is sending Vance to Hungary to meet with Prime Minister Viktor Orbán ahead of upcoming elections.

Maine Specifically Targeted: Trump called out Maine by name in a Truth Social post announcing Vance's new fraud investigation role.

Weight Comment Controversy: Trump made public remarks about Vance's weight during the fraud czar announcement.

Left vs Right: Left-leaning outlets like The Daily Beast and The Independent frame Vance's appointment as a partisan "crusade" against blue states and compare it to previous failed czar positions. Right-leaning sources such as Just The News and NDTV present the role more neutrally as a legitimate government oversight function targeting fraud and federal fund misuse.

Another busy day in the life of America's Vice President.
